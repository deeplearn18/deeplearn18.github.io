We form good habits first, and then let good habits make us.

Day by day

  1. 11:00~6:00 –Sleep Time

  2. 6:00~8:00 –Meditate, go to the bathroom, drink water, wash your face, brush your left hand, gargle, buy breakfast, eat breakfast

  3. 8:00~12:00 – Work, Learn, Think, Communicate, Cooperate

Family Day – Outdoor/Travel

  1. 12:00~14:00 – Lunch, Meditate, Noon break

  2. 14:00~18:00 –Work, Learn, Think, Communicate, Cooperate

Family Day – Outdoor/Travel

  1. 18:00~19:00 –Supper, Walk

  2. 19:00~23:00 –Play with children, bathe, entertain, sleep, Meditate

Learning

  1. Read correctly word by word
  2. Digest, Record, Practice, Review
