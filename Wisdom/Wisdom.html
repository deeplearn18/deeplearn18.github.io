  1. 1. Practice

Knowledge comes from learning, Wisdom comes from living.

Every day is your best version.

Winged Victory of Samothrace

The best work is the unfinished work.

Learned, interrogated, thoughtfully, discernible, and committed

There is no strong person in the world. In fact, we are all weak people - just different degrees of weakness, or different aspects of weakness.

Responsible for yourself, study for yourself, struggle for yourself.

Pride-Don’t take yourself too seriously.
Inferiority complex-and don’t take yourself for granted.
Pride and inferiority are often twin brothers.

Knowing is knowing, not knowing is not knowing.

“Integrity is not only an attitude and a will, but also an ability. If there is a will, but it can’t achieve results, it is not honest.”

Aim high and look far.

Too small. It is not worth mentioning.

The length of Life,
The height of the Career,
The long river of History,
The evolution of Future Generations.
